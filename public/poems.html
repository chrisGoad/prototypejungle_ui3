Reconductoring: an alternative to a new transmission line across the Caja del Rio?

Reconductoring definition:To replace a transmission cable with a conductor of higher capacity without replacing the towers

This technology has been used in thousands of projects around the world, sometimes without disrupting power.

CTC Global's explanation of the benefits of reconductoring
